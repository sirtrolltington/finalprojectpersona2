GHOST OF TSUSHIMA

This 2020 singleplayer action-adventure game set in feudal Japan follows the story of Jin Sakai, a samurai who must choose between following the honorable samurai code or use practical but dishonorable methods to defend Tsushima island against an entire army of Mongol invaders. This game features some of the most beautiful and impressive open world design I've ever had the pleasure of experiencing, going hand in hand with an excellent story and a myriad of small quests to completely immerse yourself in.
