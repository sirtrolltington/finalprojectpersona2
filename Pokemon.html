POKEMON SCARLET & VIOLET

The latest entry to the storied Pokemon franchise, Pokemon Scarlet and Violet marks the first time a mainline entry features an open world, shaking up the usual formula of progression and catching Pokemon for the better. While the game does experience the occasional graphical and technical issues, the Pokemon experience is just as fun as ever.
